# Phase 2 Human Validation: Brand and Visual Design

## What to look at

Open `demo-1B/prototype.html` in a browser at **desktop** and **phone** widths. Compare side-by-side with `demo-1B/03-brand-guidelines.html` for 2 minutes.

## Questions for the reviewer

1. Does the page feel like the **same brand** as the guidelines HTML?
2. Is the hero **premium** without feeling intimidating to beginners?
3. Are CTAs easy to spot within 3 seconds of landing?
4. Is anything visually **noisy** or off-brand (gradients, stock SaaS vibes)?
5. Are logos **clean and sparse**, not repeated excessively?

## Decisions needing human judgment

- Acid green intensity: energizing vs overwhelming.
- Uppercase headline aggression: confident vs shouty.
- Whether dark-only is right for entire page (expected: yes).

## What “good” looks like

- You’d trust this enough to enter an email for a free kit.
- Visual design supports the copy instead of fighting it.

## What would require revision

- Looks like a different product than the brand guide.
- Text hard to read in any section.
- Primary CTA does not stand out.

**Time box:** 10 minutes. Approve before Phase 3.
