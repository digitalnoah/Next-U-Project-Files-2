# Phase 5 Human Validation: Release Readiness

## What to look at

Treat this as a **final stakeholder review** before workshop demo. Test on your phone and laptop.

## Questions for the reviewer

1. **60-second test:** Open the page cold — who is it for, what do you get, what do you do?
2. On your phone, is anything frustrating to tap or read?
3. Tab through the page without a mouse — any traps or missing focus?
4. Submit the form once — still clear and honest?
5. Would you be proud showing this in a **vibe coding** class?
6. Anything you’d cut for time, or anything missing for credibility?

## Decisions needing human judgment

- Ship as-is vs one more copy tweak round.
- Whether to restore external CSS for maintainability (overrides single-file requirement — default: keep single file).

## What “good” looks like

- Cohesive product: brand + story + CTA + working prototype form.
- No code reading required to approve.

## What would require revision

- Mobile layout broken in real Safari/Chrome on device.
- Accessibility blocker (can’t complete signup with keyboard).
- Brand or trust breakdown vs earlier phases.
