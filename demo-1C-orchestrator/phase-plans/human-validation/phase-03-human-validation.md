# Phase 3 Human Validation: Full Page Story

## What to look at

Read the full page as a prospective student/founder. Do not inspect source code first.

## Questions for the reviewer

1. After one scroll, is the **free kit** the action you’d take first?
2. Do you understand what happens in the **workshop** vs what the kit gives you alone?
3. Are the **four examples** concrete enough to picture your own project?
4. Does pricing feel fair and clear (even as example pricing)?
5. Does the FAQ remove your top **two** objections?
6. Any section feel too long, redundant, or salesy?

## Decisions needing human judgment

- Whether to shorten pricing or FAQ for workshop demo length.
- Whether mid-page CTA repetition helps or annoys.

## What “good” looks like

- Complete marketing story without talking to a human.
- You’d sign up for the free kit to see the templates.

## What would require revision

- Confusion between kit, intro workshop, and full workshop.
- Missing “plan before build” message.
- Copy that sounds like every other AI course landing page.

**Time box:** 15–20 minutes. Approve before Phase 4.
