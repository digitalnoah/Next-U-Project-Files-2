# Phase 1 Human Validation: Content Architecture

## What to look at

Open `demo-1B/prototype.html` in a browser (styling may be minimal). Scroll top to bottom once without reading code.

## Questions for the reviewer

1. Within **60 seconds**, do you know who this is for and what action to take?
2. Does the hero promise feel **credible** (not hype)?
3. Is the problem section describing **your** stuck moments as a beginner?
4. Does the workshop flow (plan → build → test → improve → share) make sense?
5. Is the **free starter kit** clearly separate from paid workshops?
6. Would anything confuse a student vs a founder, or does mixed-audience copy still work?

## Decisions needing human judgment

- Copy tone: confident vs too sharp vs too soft.
- Section order: does problem → outcomes → process feel natural?
- Headline strength: is H1 the right promise?

## What “good” looks like

- Reads like a real workshop landing page, not a template.
- You would forward the link to someone with a product idea.
- No section feels like filler.

## What would require revision

- Missing or weak explanation of what Vibe Code Academy is.
- Pricing or kit contents unclear.
- FAQ does not reduce “I’m not technical enough” anxiety.
- Voice drifts into AI guru clichés.

**Time box:** 10–15 minutes. Approve to proceed to `build/phase-02-build.md`.
