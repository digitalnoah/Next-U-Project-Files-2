# Phase 4 Human Validation: Signup Flow

## What to look at

Go directly to `#signup` (or click hero CTA). Test with a real email you control mentally — e.g. `alex@example.com`.

## Questions for the reviewer

1. Is it obvious you are requesting the **free kit**, not buying a workshop?
2. After success, do you know **what you would receive** in a real product?
3. Is the **simulated** disclaimer clear and honest (not hidden)?
4. Does the workshop link feel like a natural next step?
5. Did anything feel broken or surprising?

## Test script

1. Click submit with empty field — note error clarity.
2. Enter `bad` — note error.
3. Enter valid email — read success panel fully.

## Decisions needing human judgment

- Wording of simulation disclaimer (too loud vs too subtle).
- Whether to show submitted email in thank-you (privacy comfort).

## What “good” looks like

- You’d demo this in a workshop without apologizing for the fake backend.
- Success state reinforces brand voice.

## What would require revision

- Feels like dark pattern or hidden simulation.
- Errors confusing or mean-spirited.
- Success panel visually broken on phone.

**Time box:** 10 minutes.
