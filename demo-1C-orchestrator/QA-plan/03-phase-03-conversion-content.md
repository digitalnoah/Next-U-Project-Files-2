# Phase 3 QA: Conversion content

**Build file:** `build-plan/phases/03-phase-03-conversion-content.md`  
**Detail:** `phase-plans/QA/phase-03-qa.md`

## QA goals

Verify conversion narrative, pricing clarity, and CTA wiring.

## Outcomes (check each)

- [ ] Every major section has complete, non-placeholder copy
- [ ] Free starter kit clearly easiest first step vs paid workshops
- [ ] All four example cards present with distinct titles
- [ ] Pricing tiers match planning decisions ($0 / $149 / $299 / team)
- [ ] FAQ reduces “not technical enough” anxiety
- [ ] Secondary CTA hrefs correct (`#examples`, `#pricing`, mailto placeholder)
- [ ] Repeated CTAs lead toward `#signup`
- [ ] No off-limits v1 content (video, checkout, real dates)

## Pass criteria

All outcomes with evidence.

## Fail conditions

- Missing tier, example, or FAQ topic
- Broken anchor targets
- Hype voice contradicting brand

**Tone/headline judgment** → `pass_with_risks`.
