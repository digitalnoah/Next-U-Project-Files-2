# Phase 4 QA: Signup form

**Build file:** `build-plan/phases/04-phase-04-signup-form.md`  
**Detail:** `phase-plans/QA/phase-04-qa.md`

## QA goals

Verify form UX, validation, and simulated success behavior.

## Outcomes (check each)

- [ ] Invalid email shows clear error without page reload
- [ ] Valid email shows success state (no real send)
- [ ] Success lists starter kit items from planning decisions
- [ ] Prototype / simulated delivery disclaimer visible
- [ ] Primary intent: requesting starter kit is obvious
- [ ] Post-success secondary CTA to `#pricing` works
- [ ] Labels and focus order usable keyboard-only

## Pass criteria

All outcomes with evidence (steps reproduced in QA-results).

## Fail conditions

- Form actually navigates away or posts externally
- Missing disclaimer or kit list
- Success shown without validation
